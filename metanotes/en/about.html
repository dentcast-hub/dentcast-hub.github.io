Academic Background

Dr. Foad Shahabian is a Prosthodontist and a graduate of the National Organization for Development of Exceptional Talents (NODET).

He received his Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) from Shahid Beheshti University of Medical Sciences in 2006, and completed his specialty training in Prosthodontics at Mashhad University of Medical Sciences in 2012.

Dr. Shahabian is board-certified in Prosthodontics and served as a faculty member at Ahvaz Jundishapur University of Medical Sciences from 2012 to 2017. His private clinical practice has been active since 2013.

Clinical Focus

His professional work centers on evidence-based decision-making, biological analysis of treatment planning, and achieving a coherent balance between esthetics, function, and long-term prognosis.

About DentCast

DentCast was founded in 2019 as the first Persian-language scientific dental podcast — an independent educational initiative aimed at extending academic-level learning beyond the traditional university setting.

Over the years it has become one of the most recognized platforms among Persian-speaking dentists, with thousands of active followers, and has directly inspired multiple dental podcasts across the region.

Platform Structure

The Persian edition is built around structured audio episodes, written summaries, visual educational modules, and AI-assisted scientific article analysis. The English section — MetaNotes — focuses on original clinical ideas and independent conceptual notes that go beyond article summaries and reflect the author's analytical perspective.

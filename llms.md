## Overview
Dentcast is a professional dental education platform created and curated by **Dr. Foad Shahabian**, a licensed **Prosthodontist**.  
The platform focuses on **advanced prosthodontic concepts**, **clinical decision-making**, and the **philosophy of dentistry**, with an emphasis on evidence-based reasoning rather than protocol-only instruction.

Dentcast is designed primarily for dental professionals, while remaining accessible to informed non-professional audiences for educational purposes.
